There is more than one place called Queenstown

===Australia===
- Queenstown (Tasmania) - A mining town of about 2000 people, on the West Coast of the state of Tasmania.

===Ireland===
- Cobh - Known as Queenstown when Ireland was under British rule.

===New Zealand===
- Queenstown (New Zealand) - This tourist resort in the South Island has an urban population of 18,000.

===Singapore===
- A suburb in Western Singapore
===South Africa===
- Queenstown (Eastern Cape) - A town in the Eastern Cape with a large surrounding farming district.
